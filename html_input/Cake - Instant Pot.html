Cake

We need

  • 1 cup flour

  • 1 cup water

  • ½ cup butter

  • ½ cup sugar

  • ¼ cup milk

  • 1 egg

  • 1 small spoon vanilla

  • 1 small spoon baking powder



How to bake:

  1. Mix flour and baking powder.

  2. Mix butter and sugar.

  3. Mix egg and vanilla.

  4. Slowly mix together.

  5. Mix milk together.

  6. Put butter on a pan.

  7. Pour everything in the pan.

  8. Put foil on the pan.

  9. Pour water.

  10. Put pan in.

  11. Cook for 30 minutes.

  12. Wait 10 minutes.

  13. Take out the cake.

  14. Wait. The cake is too hot.

  15. Eat the cake.
