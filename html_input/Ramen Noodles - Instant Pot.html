Ramen Noodles


We need

  • 1 pack dry ramen noodles

  • 1 1/2 cup chicken stock

  • 2 small spoon soy sauce

  • 1 small spoon sesame oil

  • 1 small spoon onion

  • 1 small spoon garlic

  • 1/2 small spoon ginger

  • 1 mushroom

  • Green onion


How to cook

  1. Pour the stock in the pot.

  2. Put the ramen noodles pack in.

  3. Put everything on top of the ramen. Put the lid on the pot.

  4. Set the cook time to 1 minute on high.

  5. Carefully open the pot.

  6. Mix the noodles.

  7. Pour the ramen soup into a bowl.

  8. Eat.
