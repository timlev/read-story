Jianzi and Tag Differences


Jianzi and tag have many differences. The biggest difference is that Jianzi uses a featherball, while tag does not require a ball. Another important difference is that Jianzi is played by kicking a ball. When playing tag, however, players use their hands to chase each other. Last, Jianzi and tag differ in the amount of running required. Jianzi does not require as much running as tag does. Because of their unique rules and equipment, Jianzi and tag are great options to add variety to recess.
