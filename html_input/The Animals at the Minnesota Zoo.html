The Animals at the Minnesota Zoo


The students at Roseville Adult Learning Center go on a field trip. Thirty-two (32) students and four (4) teachers.


We take a bus.


At the zoo, we walk.

We see the monkeys first.

Next, we see penguins.



Then we see bats in the tree.


We see many, many animals.

We like the fish and the goats and the seals and the sharks and the birds and deer.


We do not see the wolf or the lion.

But we do see the tiger under the tree.


The tiger is tired.






Then we want to go home BUT one student is missing (lost)

OH NO!!


Two men walk back to look for the student.


Teacher Tim looks on the bus.

He says The student is on the bus!

YEAH!!


The missing student is young.

He is smart.

He goes to the bus.


Teacher Tim and a student go looking for the two men.

The students and Teacher Jenn and Teacher Rebecca and Teacher Joan go to the bus.


Teacher Tim and all the men come back to the bus.


The teachers count. They count again.

They call names.


We drive back to school.

But we are late. No bus home.


Some students go to Job Club.

Some students drive home.

Some students ride with Director Tim.

Some students ride with Teacher Tim.

Some students ride with their children.

Some students ride with Teacher Jenn.

Everyone is okay.

We like the zoo.


THE END
