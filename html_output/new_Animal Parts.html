Alligator

An alligator has a big mouth.

It has many teeth

and a big tongue.

It has a long tail.

It has scales.



Butterfly

A butterfly has a long, thin body.

It has two colorful wings.

It has two antennae on its head.

This butterfly has spots

on its wings.



Chimpanzee

A chimpanzee has long arms.

It has fingers on its hands.

It has toes on its feet.

A chimpanzee has lots of hair.



Elephant

An elephant has big ears.

It has two long tusks.

It has a long trunk.

An elephant has four big feet.



Lion

A lion has fur.

It has four big paws with claws.

This lion has a big mane

around its head.

It has two eyes.



Parrot

A parrot has two wings.

It has a long tail.

It has colorful feathers

It has a beak.

It has claws on its feet.



Tortoise

A tortoise has a hard shell.

It has four legs and four feet.

A tortoise can make its head

and legs go into its shell.



Shark

A shark has fins.

It has two fins on its back

and two big fins on the side.

It has other fins too.

It uses its fins and tail to move

in the water.

It uses its gills to breathe

under water.
